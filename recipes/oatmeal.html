Oatmeal

Description

Do you have depression, or maybe ED with a splash of orthorexia? Well oh boy, do we have a treat for you!

With baked oatmeal you can run with autopilot on and still get proper nutrition. And if you make it with water and tears, your little ED heart will sing! Yummy And kinda salty... Stay with us to learn to cook this staple breakfast.

Ingredients

  • oats
  • water or milk
  • berries
  • almonds

Steps

  1. add liquid and oats in a pan
  2. cook for 5 minutes
  3. in a bowl put berries on the bottom
  4. pour cooked oats on top
  5. garnish with berries and almonds
  6. move thru the rest of the day like a zombie
